How Ditherr Works

1. Add Ditherr to your website

SIgn up to Ditherr, it's free to use on your website. Once signed up, copy the required code into the head of your website html.

Add the Ditherr tag to the form or event you want your users to complete, your almost ready to stop the Dither.

2. Time to tempt your users

Head over to your Ditherr account panel and select the kind of incentives you want to tempt your users with.

You can show users discounts on products and services they have already looked at (this works just like Google Ads Display Network), or show them incentives taylored to your website.

Not sure? Don't panic. By default Ditherr will show incentives that work well for you and your users.

3. Watch your stats soar

With Ditherr working on your site in important areas (sign up forms, surveys etc) you will see bounces decrease and Goals increase.

Ditherr helps push users over the line by offering them a little extra for their effort and time.

You see an increase in your important Goals on the site, Advertisers reach out to interested parties and users get a little something for their time, its a Win Win Win!
